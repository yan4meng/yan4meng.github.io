Yan Meng （孟岩）

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests include IoT system security and privacy including wireless security, voice interface security, and IoT application security & privacy. I am the recipient of the 2022 ACM China Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award and the Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program by CAST (2023-2025).
孟岩，上海交通大学计算机科学与工程系助理研究员，研究方向：物联网安全，获得2022年ACM中国优博奖，入选2023年度中国科协青年人才托举工程。
