Yan Meng （孟岩）

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests include IoT system security and privacy including wireless security, voice interface security, and IoT application security & privacy. I am the recipient of the 2022 China Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award.
